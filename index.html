About the Garden

☙

Hello. I am an independent thinker and student at IISER Kolkata.

I built this digital garden as an antidote to algorithmic feeds and the decline of platforms like Quora. This is my personal archive. No likes, no metrics, just a space for deep thought, scientific inquiry, and raw observation.

Feel free to wander through the essays, research notes, or the scrapbook. There is no algorithm here, only curiosity.

"In an age of infinite noise, the quietest corner of the web is the most radical. Here I cultivate thought like a garden — slowly, deliberately, for no one but myself."
